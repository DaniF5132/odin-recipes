Cheescake

Description

An easy and tasty recipe of cheescake — that’s it!

The amount of sugar used in the recipe can be modified in function of the cook

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 6 cheese portions
  • 1 natural yogurt
  • sugar
  • flour
  • milk
  • 200m cooking cream

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees
  2. Beat the eggs
  3. Add the cheese portions
  4. Add the yougurt and use the container as a mesure for the other ingredits
  5. Add 1 container and half of sugar
  6. Add 1 container of flour
  7. Add 1 container of milk
  8. Add the cream
  9. Mix everything with a blender
  10. Put in the oven for around 40 minuts
